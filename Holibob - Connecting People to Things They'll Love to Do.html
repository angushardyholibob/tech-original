FAQs for Travel Brands

How much time will it take
to integrate and manage?

We can deliver a custom booking experience - designed to your brand standards - in a matter of weeks. And we’ve built the marketing aspect of our Experiences Marketing Platform around industry-standard technologies that should be simple and efficient to connect with your existing infrastructure. Achieving the level of personalisation we aspire to will depend on the accessibility of data, naturally this means integration’s can take varying amounts of time.

Do you handle booking operations?

We handle everything from taking payments, to unexpected events and handling cancellation requests, all through our customer service team.

Can I pick which destinations
and products I want to sell?

Absolutely, we’ll help you to showcase the products and destinations that align with your brand, and crucially - your customers will want to book.

Why do you share in profits
rather than charging fees?

This is probably the question we get more than any other, and the answer is simple: We believe the best way to grow your business is a true partnership in which you're able to put your expertise and energy intorunning great tours, and we put 100% of our focus on driving direct bookings - not billing hours. Together, wewill succeed.

Are you GDPR compliant?

We’ve built our system around industry-standard technologies and with a close eye on data protection and privacy. If need be, we can work with your legal team to ensure they are happy with our approach.
